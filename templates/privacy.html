Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated December 9, 2025

Welcome to UniversityTuitionCompare.com ("we," "us," "our").

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit our website at https://www.universitytuitioncompare.com. The applicable privacy law may give you specific rights in relation to your personal information, depending on your geographic location.

By using the Site, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We aim to keep our data practices simple and transparent. We collect the following types of information:

Information You Provide Voluntarily

You may choose to provide:

  • Contact details such as your name and email address (e.g., when submitting enquiries or subscribing to updates).
  • Feedback or messages you send through contact forms or email.

We do not request or collect sensitive personal information (e.g., health data, financial data, government IDs).

Information Collected Automatically

When you access the Site, we may automatically collect:

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and general location (city/region)
  • Pages viewed, links clicked, time spent on pages
  • Cookies or similar technologies

This information helps us keep the Site reliable, secure, and easy to use.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies help us improve your browsing experience. We use:

  • Essential cookies (support basic functionality)
  • Analytics cookies (understand usage patterns)
  • Performance cookies (improve site speed and reliability)

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information only for clear, legitimate purposes, including:

  • Operating and improving the Site
  • Responding to your enquiries or support requests
  • Monitoring Site performance and detecting technical issues
  • Analyzing aggregate trends to improve our comparison tools
  • Complying with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

Depending on your location, we process personal data under one or more of the following lawful bases:

  • Consent – When you voluntarily provide information (e.g., subscription forms).
  • Legitimate interests – To operate, secure, and improve our Site.
  • Legal obligation – When required to comply with applicable laws.

4. How We Share Information

We only share information when necessary and with appropriate protections in place.

We may share information with:

  • Trusted service providers (e.g., analytics platforms or hosting providers)
  • Legal authorities, if required to comply with law or protect our rights

We do not:

  • Sell personal data
  • Share personal data with advertisers
  • Transfer data to countries lacking adequate data protection without safeguards

All third parties are required to handle data securely and in line with GDPR standards.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law.

In general:

  • Contact form submissions: up to 12 months
  • Analytics data: varies based on provider retention policies (typically 12–26 months)
  • Server logs: up to 12 months, unless security concerns require longer storage

You may request deletion at any time (see Your Rights below).

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)

To exercise your rights, contact us at:

info@universitytuitioncompare.com

We may need to verify your identity before responding.

7. Keeping Your Information Secure

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Secure hosting infrastructure
  • Access controls and audit logs
  • Regular maintenance and monitoring

However, no online service can guarantee 100% security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Site is intended for general audiences and not targeted at children under 16.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If you believe a child has submitted information, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Links to Third-Party Websites

The Site may link to third-party university websites or external resources.

We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before interacting with them.
